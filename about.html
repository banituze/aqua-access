Brief Bio

My journey in technology began with a passion for solving problems and creating solutions that make a difference. As an ALX SE Tutor, I have had the opportunity to support and mentor learners in the Software Engineering Program, facilitating 1-to-1 tutoring sessions and contributing to a collaborative learning environment.

By combining technical expertise with a deep commitment to environmental responsibility and community development, AquaAccess seeks to deliver scalable solutions that strengthen resilience, promote public health, and foster long-term sustainability.
